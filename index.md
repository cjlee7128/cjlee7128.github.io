  <h1 class="hero-name">Changjae Lee</h1>
  <div class="hero-title">Ph.D. Student in Computer Science & Engineering</div>
  
  <p class="hero-bio">
    I am a Ph.D. candidate researcher at the <strong>University at Buffalo (UB)</strong> under the supervision of <strong><a href="https://www.xlab-ub.com/" target="_blank">Prof. Jinjun Xiong</a></strong>. My research focuses on developing intelligent systems at the intersection of <strong>Human-AI Interaction</strong>, <strong>databases</strong>, and <strong>agentic AI</strong>. 
  </p>
  
  <p class="hero-bio" style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-top: -1rem;">
    My research is driven by the challenge of bridging <strong>Human-AI Interaction</strong> and advanced <strong>database systems</strong>. Through <strong>Spark</strong>, a multimodal human-robot interaction system, I design agentic AI environments that enhance usability and support natural-language problem decomposition. Through <strong>SABER</strong>, an extensible, SQL-compatible semantic data processing system, I develop unified architectures that integrate relational databases with LLM-based workloads. Together, this work aims to create highly interactive, scalable, and intuitive platforms that simplify AI integration into real-world tasks.
  </p>
